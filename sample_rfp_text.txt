REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL — Cloud Analytics Platform  
Issued by: GlobalTech Enterprises  
Response Deadline: [14 business days from receipt]  

Introduction:  
GlobalTech Enterprises is a leading provider of innovative technology solutions, dedicated to helping businesses optimize their operations through advanced analytics. We are seeking a cloud analytics platform vendor that can provide robust capabilities for data analysis, visualization, and reporting to support our data-driven decision-making processes.

Questions:

1. **Security**
   1.1 What security protocols do you have in place to protect data stored in your cloud analytics platform?  
   1.2 How do you ensure the security of data in transit between our systems and your platform?  
   1.3 Can you provide details on your incident response plan and how you handle data breaches?

2. **Technical Architecture**
   2.1 Describe the architecture of your cloud analytics platform. How does it support scalability and performance?  
   2.2 What integrations do you offer with existing data sources and third-party tools? Please provide examples.  
   2.3 How does your platform handle real-time data processing and analytics?

3. **Data Privacy**
   3.1 What measures do you take to comply with global data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA)?  
   3.2 How do you manage data ownership and access rights for our organization within your platform?

4. **Implementation**
   4.1 What is your typical timeline for implementing your cloud analytics platform from start to finish?  
   4.2 What support and training do you provide during the implementation phase to ensure a smooth transition?

5. **Pricing**
   5.1 Can you outline your pricing model, including any tiered options based on usage or features?  
   5.2 Are there additional costs for support, maintenance, or upgrades? Please provide a detailed breakdown.
